Our Relocation to Silicon Valley: A Tale of Urgency and Adaptation by Mikhail P.

Years ago, I first came to Silicon Valley as a Russian businessman, alone and focused solely on opportunities in tech innovation. However, the escalating political tensions back home made it necessary for my entire family to join me here last year. This move wasn't planned leisurely but was a sudden necessity.

When my wife, and our three children arrived last year, they faced immense challenges. None of them spoke English, which immediately isolated them in our new community. I was consumed by my work, the only source of income for our family, leaving me with no time to navigate the daunting maze of legal and bureaucratic processes required for our new life....
